About

I remember the first time I wrote a line of code. I was intimidated, excited, and most of all, intrigued. The intrigue that came from being able to create something exciting and expressive of who I am, simply from typing, led to my passion for web development. As a front-end web developer, I am constantly looking for ways to express myself. For my clients, it is my goal to express how they want to be seen by the public through their website. I love what I do and I hope we can work together soon!
